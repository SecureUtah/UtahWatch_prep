UtahWatch - Civic

UtahWatch tracks the HTTPS support of websites in Utah. Click the Plus icon next to each name to see the details of that site’s HTTPS support and to send them a tweet about how they rate. If you don't see the Twitter button then your browser or your ad-blocker has prevented it from loading.

Please see the About page for more information.
