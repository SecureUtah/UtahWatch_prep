UtahWatch - Healthcare

UtahWatch tracks the HTTPS support of websites in Utah. Click the Plus icon next to each name to show details about that site’s HTTPS support and then send them a tweet about how they rate. If you don't see the Twitter button you probably have an ad-blocker installed.

Please see the About page for more information.
